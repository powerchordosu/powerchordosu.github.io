About

Established in 2013, Power Chord is Oregon State University's all-inclusive A cappella group. The members of the group represent a variety of majors, but share a common love of making music. Come watch Power Chord sing every Friday on the MU steps with Outspoken and Divine! 5 PM on Friday!

John

John is a junior studying ocean science and this is his third year in Power Chord. He is also the group’s president! Origionally from Arizona, John joined Power Chord because he has been singing in musical groups his entire life and he wanted to continue that beyond high school. Outside of the group, John works as a lab technician for OSU and runs a community service non-profit based in Corvallis. Also, John is great at certain impressions. Ask him about Scooby and Shaggy!

  • Position: President

Maddie

Maddie is a sophomore studying choral education and this is her second year in Power Chord. This year, she is serving as the group’s Music Director! Maddie joined Power Chord because she has been singing since she could remember and she knew music had to be part of her college experience. Power Chord was the perfect fit! As the music directorm Maddie arranges many of the songs we sing throughout the year, a difficult but very rewarding job as she will tell you. Maddie loves to bake, knit, drink tea, and hang out with her cats. She is essentially the group grandma! Fun fact: She says Ay yai yai too much.

  • Position: Music Director
  • Voice: Mezzo/Soprano
  • Major: Music
